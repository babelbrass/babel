BABEL BRASS BAND

 

Babel Brass Band is the UK's most energetic award-winning brass band mixing pop covers, D&B, funk, hip-hop & jazz!

 

Formed in 2021, Babel Brass Band has been making waves on the European and UK music scene with their dynamic live performances. They have already delivered close to 100 performances at numerous festivals and venues captivating audiences with their high-energy music and engaging stage presence including Camp Bestival, Boardmasters Festival, Brighton Fringe Festival, Alexandra Palace Festival, Islander Festival, Isle of Wight Rhythm Tree Festival, Meatopia Festival, Spraoi International Festival, All Points East Festival, Ally Pally Fireworks Festival, Sparkle in the Park Festival, London Remixed Festival, and many more.

What sets the Babel Brass Band apart from other brass bands is their commitment to creating a diverse and inclusive community through their music. They believe that music has the power to unite people from all walks of life, and they strive to make their music accessible to everyone. With their infectious energy and positive message, the Babel Brass Band is not to be missed.

Babel Brass Band has recently been named “The most unique wedding & event brass band in 2023” by the prestigious Greater London Enterprise Awards and are finalist of the 2023 UK London Remixed Brass Off, a testament to their ability to entertain crowds of all kinds.

Their original arrangements, gorgeous jazz harmonies, and stunning improvised solos are already entertaining crowds wherever they play! Their performances often include collaborations with rappers, vocalists, and DJs and have repertoire adjustable for any occasion - from large festivals to street parties, club performances, corporate functions, and weddings.
