FESTIVALS & GIGS

 

EVENTSFESTIVALS & GIGS

 

Babel Brass Band is primarily a festival band. Playing for large audiences is what they love and do best. They have showcased their energetic and dynamic performances at many UK festivals, including Boardmasters, Brighton Fringe, Camp Bestival, All Points East, Spraoi International, London Remixed, Meatopia, Alexandra Palace Festival, Islander Festival, Ally Pally Fireworks Festival, Sparkle in the Park Festival, Battersea Park Summer Festival, Rhythm Tree Festival, and Sundown Session, among others.

As seasoned performers, they understand the unique vibes of a festival setting and create unforgettable, electrifying moments that leave their audiences buzzing with energy. Their impressive roster of previous performances provides solid proof of their expertise in thrilling festivalgoers and cultivating an exhilarating atmosphere. With their eclectic blend of music styles, Babel Brass Band provides a sonic feast that caters to diverse tastes. Their unique style incorporates elements from traditional New Orleans music, contemporary pop, and funk covers, underpinned with a strong influence of DnB, Hip hop, and Jazz.

This captivating blend of sounds with powerful improvised solos has attracted the attention of a vast audience, with its fresh, contemporary twist on the brass band genre. The band often elevates their performance by collaborating with a rapper (MC) and DJ, adding another layer of auditory pleasure to their set.
